About Us

At NEXUS AQUARIUM we deliver a unique underwater experience to all our visitors. We are one of the prime centers for tourism, research and education in the great city of Washington.
Our vast tanks hold over 2 million gallons of water and are home to a wide variety of sea creatures ranging from Catfish to Crocodiles, Piranhas to Terrapins, and different species of Shark and Sea Turtle.
A tour through our facilities promises an awe inspiring experience that will not be forgotten for a lifetime. Expert tour guides are on hand to pilot our guests through this amazing journey.

NEXUS AQUARIUM engages and educates students via our specially packaged events, and is the perfect destination for a school field trip. Researchers also find here a great environment for an up close study of our marine life.
We're located at 2847 University Street, Seattle Washington.
