Check out our Events

Guided Tour

Be thrilled by the ultimate experience of literarily walking under the sea! Come and take a full tour of our facility today. Expert tour guides will lead you through wide viewing halls and winding underwater tunnels, to see our wide variety of exotic creatures. Complimentary souvenirs and other fun activities are inclusive.
Booking Fee: $40 per person

School Field Trip

Take your students on an educational adventure and explore the underwater world! This event package includes animal sightings, feeding time participation, quiz sessions with prizes, complimentary souvenir for all, and a dedicated tour guide to answer all questions and ensure the kids have a memorable outing.
Booking Fee: $25 per student (free entry for two teachers only)

Family Funtime

The family that has fun together, remains together. Take a day out with your family to visit us and enjoy our specially packaged Family Fun Tour! This includes a full tour of the facility, dolphin acrobatics, time out with penguins, and complimentary souvenir for all.
Booking Fee: $40 per adult and $30 per child below 18yrs old.

Wedding Reservation

We have the perfect setting for your wedding ceremonies! Delight your guests and loved ones by hosting your event in our serene atmosphere. A brief tour for guests is included in the package, as well as unique photo shoots for the couple.
Booking Fee: $100 per hour (maximum of 150 guests)

Business and Pleasure

Host you corporate events and end of year parties with us today. The soothing ambience of our facility is the perfect atmosphere for relaxing nerves and mingling with colleagues. Fun activities such as dolphin display, seal volleyball, and lots more are also included.
Booking Fee: $100 per person

Research Studies

We accomodate a wide array of marine animals in their simulated natural habitat, which is an ideal setting for carrying out scientific research. Researchers and Scientists are welcome to come study them. Diving gear and canoe rides are available for research work only.
Booking Fee: $500 per day
